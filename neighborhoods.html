Ashburn Loudoun County • 24 Active Listings

The heart of Loudoun County's tech corridor. Top-rated schools, master-planned communities like Brambleton and Broadlands, and easy access to the Dulles Greenway. Perfect for families seeking suburban comfort with urban amenities.

Chantilly Fairfax County • 18 Active Listings

A vibrant community at the intersection of Routes 50 and 28. Home to diverse dining, excellent schools in the FCPS system, and neighborhoods ranging from established colonials to new construction. Great value for Fairfax County.

Median: $680K Schools: 8/10 Commute: 30 min

Aldie Loudoun County • 12 Active Listings

Where Virginia horse country meets modern upscale living. Communities like Willowsford and Stone Ridge offer estate-style homes with breathtaking views. Equestrian estates, vineyards, and rolling hills create an unmatched quality of life.

Median: $850K Schools: 9/10 Commute: 45 min
→
Centreville Fairfax County • 15 Active Listings

One of NOVA's best-kept secrets for value. Family-friendly communities like Virginia Run and Centre Ridge offer spacious homes, top schools, and easy access to I-66. Rich in Civil War history with modern conveniences.

Median: $600K Schools: 8/10 Commute: 35 min
→
Leesburg Loudoun County • 20 Active Listings

Historic charm meets modern growth. Downtown Leesburg's shops and restaurants complement new communities like Lansdowne and River Creek. A strong sense of community with festivals, farmers markets, and the W&OD trail.

Median: $720K Schools: 8/10 Commute: 45 min
→
South Riding Loudoun County • 16 Active Listings

A master-planned community with resort-style amenities. Multiple pools, fitness centers, and miles of trails. Award-winning Loudoun County schools and a tight-knit community feel make this ideal for growing families.

Median: $700K Schools: 9/10 Commute: 40 min
→
Brambleton Loudoun County • Amenity-Rich Living

A planned community built around trails, pools, schools, and a town-center rhythm. Buyers like Brambleton when they want convenience and neighborhood energy without sacrificing home quality.

Broadlands Loudoun County • Established Community

A quieter, greener Loudoun option with mature landscaping, trails, and a steady move-up buyer profile. Broadlands appeals when buyers want polish without the busiest pace.

One Loudoun Loudoun County • Live-Work-Play
