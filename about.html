My Story

Real Estate Is More Than a Transaction

I believe real estate is more than just a transaction — it's a life-changing journey. With over a decade of experience specializing in Fairfax, Loudoun, and Prince William Counties, I've had the privilege of guiding hundreds of families through one of the most important decisions of their lives.

Energetic, fun-loving, and customer driven, I'm dedicated to helping my clients close the best deal possible. As a NOVA realtor with deep roots in the community, whether you're buying your first home or selling your home, I bring honest guidance, deep market knowledge, and relentless advocacy to every relationship.

Together with my business partner Ram, we work collaboratively to ensure both sellers obtain maximum returns and buyers negotiate the most favorable terms. We handle everything personally — from property auditing and staging to strategic pricing and negotiation — because the details make the difference.

When I'm not helping clients find their dream homes, you'll find me in my garden in Aldie, working on landscape design, perfecting interior decoration, or spending quality time with my husband and two children. I believe that a balanced life — meditation, exercise, and family — makes me a better advocate for my clients.

My Process

How I Work

For Sellers: It starts with a complimentary consultation where we discuss your goals, timeline, and property. I then personally audit and stage your home, develop a strategic pricing approach, and leverage my network to find qualified buyers. My track record? Signed contracts in as little as one week.

For Buyers: I listen first. I want to understand not just what you need in a home, but how you want to live. Then I use my deep knowledge of Northern Virginia communities to match you with neighborhoods that fit your lifestyle. Throughout the process, I provide timely information and expert guidance at every step.

Everything I do is intended to make a difference in your life so that you can buy and sell with confidence. I'm here 24/7 to assist you from start to finish.
